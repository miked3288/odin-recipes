Carbquik Sausage Balls

  • Prep: 15 mins
  • Cook: 20 minutes
  • Total: 35 minutes
  • Servings: 30 balls

These are good for breakfast, dinner, or just a snack. These Keto Sausage balls gives the comfort of a biscuit without the guilt.

This recipe uses a low carb Bisquik substitution called Carbquik. Carbquik is a good flour and breading alternative, especially if you are allergic to nuts and cannot eat almond flour or do not want a coconut flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Carbquik
  • 1⁄2 lb bulk sausage
  • 2 cups (8oz) cheddar cheese
  • 1⁄4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1⁄8 cup water
  • 1⁄8 cup heavy cream
  • a dash of itallian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease a large pan.
  3. Stir all ingredients by hand in a bowl. It will be dry at first but keeping working it and it will get moist.
  4. Roll into 30 large balls.
  5. Bake til brown appx. 15-20 minutes.
