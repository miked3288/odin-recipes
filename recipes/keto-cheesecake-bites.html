Keto Cheesecake Bites

  • Prep: 12 minutes
  • Cook: 20 minutes
  • Resting Time: 20 minutes
  • Total: 52 minutes
  • Servings: 12

Most of the ingredients in cheesecake are already keto friendly with the only exception being the sugar. With this super simple recipe, we will make cheesecake bites that are out of this world.

Ingredients

  • 2 8oz blocks of cream cheese, softened
  • 1⁄2 cup powdered erythritol
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Softened cream cheese in the microwave until just softened.
  3. Add all ingredients to the cream cheese and mix on medium/high until smooth.
  4. Line muffin tins with parchment paper muffin liners and pour equal amounts of batter into each cup
  5. Bake for 18-20 minutes. DO NOT OVERBAKE!
  6. Let cool for 20 minutes
  7. Top with favor toppings i.e. Fruit, whipped cream, keto friendly ice cream, etc.
