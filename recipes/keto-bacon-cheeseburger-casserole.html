Keto Bacon Cheeseburger Casserole

  • Prep: 15 mins
  • Cook: 35 mins
  • Total: 50mins
  • Servings: 12

Trying to cut the carbs but don't want to cut the flavor? Then this super easy to make Keto Bacon Cheeseburger Casserole is for you!

With only 2g of carbs preserving and a whopping 12 servings a batch, this can feed a big family or can be an easy lunch meal prep for a week!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 pound cooked bacon, cut into pieces
  • 8 eggs
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon salt
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 ounces shredded cheddar cheese divded

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Chop and brown beef with garlic and onion powder for 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease.
  3. Evenly spread beef onto the bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole pan and stir in bacon.
  4. Whisk eggs, heavy cream, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl until well combined. Stir in 8oz of the cheddar cheese.
  5. Pour egg mixture over the beef and bacon and top with the remaining 4 ounces cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, 30 to 35 minutes.
